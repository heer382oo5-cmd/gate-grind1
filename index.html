TOPPER PRINCIPLES
📖
PYQ IS YOUR BIBLE
Source: AIR 1–50 strategies
  • Solve 15+ years of PYQs for every subject — minimum twice.
  • GATE repeats patterns. The solver of old papers always wins.
  • Understand every wrong option, not just the right answer.
  • Time yourself in exam conditions. 3 mins/question maximum.
🧠
CONCEPTS FIRST, PROBLEMS SECOND
Topper methodology
  • Never jump to PYQs without clearing theory. It will backfire.
  • Standard References: CLRS (Algorithms), Tanenbaum (OS/Networks).
  • Write handwritten notes — GATE toppers unanimously agree on this.
  • Revise each subject 3 times: overview → deep → flash review.
⏰
TIME IS YOUR ONLY WEAPON
Discipline over talent
  • 9.5 hours of FOCUSED study daily beats 14 hours of distracted effort.
  • Protect your Study Block III at all costs — afternoon is where rank is decided.
  • Phone in another room during study blocks. Non-negotiable.
  • Sleep 7–8 hrs. Memory consolidates only during deep sleep.
📊
MOCK TESTS = RANK
Last 60 days strategy
  • Give full-length mocks in real exam conditions from Month 4 onwards.
  • Analyse every test: error log, time per question, weak sections.
  • Target 55+ in mocks before the real exam. Cut-off for AIR <100 is ~65.
  • Don't start new topics in the last 3 weeks. Only revise.
